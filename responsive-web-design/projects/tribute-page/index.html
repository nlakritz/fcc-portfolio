Stephen Hillenburg (1961-2018)

The True Ocean Master

Here are some quick and interesting facts about Mr. Hillenburg!

  • He started off as a marine biology teacher, which inspired him to create a cartoon about underwater creatures.
  • His first job as an animator was on Rocko's Modern Life.
  • He enjoyed being around the ocean through scuba diving, surfing, and snorkeling.
  • He created the United Plankton Charitable trust, which donates over $500,000 annually to various organizations.
  • His main achievement, SpongeBob SquarePants, has been nominated for 17 Emmy Awards.

More information about the life and achievements of Stephen Hillenburg can be found on his official Wikipedia page.
